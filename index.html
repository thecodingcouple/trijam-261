Famous Blunders

In Famous Blunders you are replaying the final moments before a chess player blundered a game piece. The objective of the game is to identify which piece was blundered and where it was moved to.

It is 's turn to move (). To select the blunder:

  1. Click on the square of game piece to move.
  2. Click on a second square to highlight it's destination.
